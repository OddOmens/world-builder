The four Great Dungeons:

- [[The City Dungeon]] — beneath [[Redel]], an open plains city with fluctuating warm and cold temperatures. Primary inhabitants: standard [[Goblin|Goblins]].
- [[The Veiled Dungeon]] — in a forest roughly a half-day's journey from [[Storford]]. Primary inhabitants: [[River Goblin|River Goblins]].
- [[The Sunken Dungeon]] — in a marsh just off the city of [[Besnor]]. Primary inhabitants: [[Moss Goblin|Moss Goblins]].
- [[Mountain Dungeon]] (unnamed) — at the base of a snowy mountain near [[Winnmer]]. Primary inhabitants: [[Frost Goblin|Frost Goblins]] (also called Cold Goblins).
